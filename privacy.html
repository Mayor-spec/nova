Data Protection Declaration

Effective: June 05, 2026. Explicit disclosure of information gathering limitations and platform encryption rules.

1. System Telemetry Aggregation

NovaTech Solutions limits inbound platform telemetry to standard protocol logs transmitted by user browsers. This metrics collection maps browser viewport structures and engine string headers exclusively to maintain asset scaling efficiency.

2. Application Caching Controls

Our infrastructure modules place short-duration storage cookies exclusively to prevent automated request flooding and protect form inputs. We do not transmit tracking identifiers to third-party ad networks.

3. Client Data Retention Boundaries

Information submitted through our pipeline forms stays completely isolated within our local databases. This information is retained solely to address system testing parameters or process talent applications, and is purged immediately upon client request.

4. Cryptographic Enforcement

Every stored text node and active transmission field is heavily isolated using industry-standard modern transport layer protocols, preventing external data inspection or tampering attempts.
